# MMPT Marketing Build Plan

## Phase 1: Foundation

- Lock the red and black design system.
- Establish shared layout, navigation, CTA, and footer components.
- Centralize content and booking links in `lib/content/site.ts`.
- Keep tracking active for page views, CTA clicks, and lead forms.

## Phase 2: Core Conversion Pages

- Homepage
- Services
- Contact

These pages should answer the main conversion questions first:

- What does MMPT offer?
- Who is it for?
- Why should I trust it?
- What should I do next?

## Phase 3: Trust Pages

- About
- Team
- FAQ
- Process

These pages deepen credibility and help reduce hesitation before booking.

## Phase 4: Data and Operations

- Send lead captures into the real CRM or database.
- Track which service interest appears most often.
- Track which CTA source leads to the most bookings.
- Use this data to refine page order, CTA copy, and follow-up workflows.

## Phase 5: Content Expansion

- Add real service detail pages.
- Add team bios and credentials.
- Add proof, testimonials, and athlete stories.
- Add SEO content for high-intent search terms.
